What is Opium?

Opium is an American record label founded in 2019 by Playboi Carti. Notable artists signed to Opium include American rappers Ken Carson and Destroy Lonely, who are both concurrently signed to Interscope Records.
Who is Playboi Carti?

Jordan Terrell Carter (born September 13, 1996), known professionally as Playboi Carti, is an American rapper. Carter was initially signed to local underground label Awful Records prior to signing with ASAP Mob's AWGE Label under Interscope Records.[9] After gaining a cult following early in his career, Carter garnered mainstream attention in 2017.

    Destroy Lonely
    Bobby Wardel Sandimanie III (born July 30, 2001), known professionally as Destroy Lonely, is an American rapper. He is best known for his debut studio album If Looks Could Kill (2023). Destroy Lonely signed to Playboi Carti's "Opium" label through Interscope Records and Ingrooves in early 2021 and is often affiliated with his labelmate Ken Carson. The Rolling Stone magazine cites him as being characterized by his "dynamic yet boring" instrumentals and his unique aesthetic consisting of dark attire and designer clothes.
